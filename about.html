About Mekezo

Premium Car Accessories for Every Need

Mekezo is a leading provider of high-quality car accessories in Ethiopia, dedicated to enhancing your driving experience through comfort, safety, and style. Founded by Amir Kedir, Mekezo specializes in importing and distributing premium car accessories that cater to the diverse needs of car owners across the country.

Our mission is to offer products that not only improve the functionality of your vehicle but also elevate its aesthetic appeal. From ergonomic seat cushions and advanced dashcams to stylish alloy wheels and protective car covers, Mekezo ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and durability.

Under the visionary leadership of Amir Kedir, Mekezo has grown to become a trusted name in the automotive accessories industry. Amir's passion for innovation and commitment to customer satisfaction drive our continuous efforts to bring the best products to the Ethiopian market.
